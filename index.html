Transforming Analogue City Council to the New

Personalised

Personalised user experience across all ACC citizen facing channels (website, customer portal, mobile phone app). A digital front door will create a more simplified and connected user experience with Analogue City Council. This will improve citizen satisfaction and quality of life.

Data Driven Analytics

Analytics combined with Artificial Intelligence will capture information about the community and deliver insightful and useful data. Data driven analytics can be integrated into Analogue City Council to provide more effective and efficient services to citizens.

Artificially Intelligent Analogue City

Artificial Intelligence will automate many repetitive, labour intensive interactions that consume Analogue City Council employees so that they can focus on the more important tasks.

Analytics and Artificial Intelligence can be easily integrated into Analogue City Council through a number of system modifications. Sufficient training and knoweledge will be available to Analogue City Council employees and citizens to gradually transform them to the new.

Who is Cynthia

Our virtual assistant, Cynthia, can help answer your Analogue City Council enquiries. She’s the new face of Analogue City Council, purpose built to help improve engagement between ACC and the community.

How can Cynthia help

Cynthia is available 24/7 to answer your Analogue City Council questions. Simply ask Cynthia your question and she will provide you with the information you're looking for. 

What can Cynthia do

Cynthia will be your first point of contact for: rate payment and information, complaint submission and tracking, planning and development applications, dog registration, health and hygiene licenses, park and facility booking, community engagement information, council asset maintenance issues, and waste collection inquiries.
